Tower 28

Long Island City
Tower 28 is renowned for its unique look and feel. Tasked to bring residents with a luxurious living experience, Whitehall brought a variety of styles to elevate user experience. Inspired by Long Island City's rich manufacturing history, each unit compliments the area's historic charm with uncompromising elegance.
Project info
Close

With the use of an industrial palette, accents of black metal contrast against lighter stone materials offering heightened sophistication. From finish to edge, stainless steel appliances compliment Quartz countertops amidst a backdrop of floor to ceiling windows. The use of these luxurious finishes bring residential rental design to the next level.

At first glance, a 57-story residential tower with 451 rental units and a 60th floor observation deck would be quite impressive, but with a standalone amenity suite with a fitness center, spa, pool, and yoga studio, the building continues to raise the bar. Whitehall’s vision literally extended traditional dimensions and broke the fourth wall into an uninterrupted versatile breezeway that features collapsible doors that open during the warmer months. All around, this incredibly rare luxury rental building with 360-degree views of the city is an unparalled experience.
