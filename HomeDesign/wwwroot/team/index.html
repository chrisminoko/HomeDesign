Seasoned professionals in design and construction

At Whitehall Interiors, we strive to create a vision that exceeds the conventional boundaries of interior design. Our team is comprised of dedicated and talented professionals who are committed to providing superior service and attention to our clients. We have the capacity to work with clients, with both local and far-reaching properties, and utilize video and screen sharing technologies that allow real time access to project data in a collaborative setting.

We have a design team to take charge of the design process every step of the way, and a production team that realize the conceptual design into thorough and detailed working drawings.

Sara Ianniciello

Senior Associate / Director of Design

As Director of Design at Whitehall Interiors, Sara Ianniciello leads a studio of talented designers and architects to conceptualize and produce elegant and original solutions for clients. Her talent and sophisticated sense of design and scale are grounded in an extensive portfolio that spans almost 20 years of interior design and architectural projects in the U.S. and abroad. Specializing in bespoke and luxury hospitality, her background lends a unique vision to the multi-family residential and commercial fields where her notoriety has brought award-winning designs to fruition.

Prior to joining Whitehall, Sara held leadership positions at firms across the U.S., where she worked on a variety of notable projects including, but not limited to 125 Greenwich, Lantern House and Mandarin Oriental Residences in New York City, Loews Atlanta, and Hyatt Regency Bethesda. Holding a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interior Architecture and Design from the Academy of Art in San Francisco, Sara is a certified Interior Designer in the State of New York, NCIDQ certified, and a professional member of IIDA.

Orlando Rodriguez

Associate / Head of Production

As the Head of Production at Whitehall Interiors, Orlando brings over 13 years of experience in residential, commercial, and retail interior design. He believes in marrying a passion for timeless and modern design and his proficiency of construction documentation consistently resulting in a finished and polished end product.

At Whitehall, Orlando is the Head of the Production, and manages a talented team to produce the highest quality of contract documentation. With proficiency and experience in all phases of design and construction on projects in the US and abroad, Orlando brings an extensive interior design and architectural knowledge base to the Whitehall team.

Orlando holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Architecture from Temple University in Philadelphia.

Janet Thai

Senior Project Manager

As a Senior Project Manager at Whitehall Interiors, Janet has over 15 years of experience that range from high-end private and multi-family residential to workplace design and mixed use developments in New York City. Janet has managed and coordinated the completion of Ocean Dreams and the Sales Gallery of Skyline Tower. Her current projects includes Skyline Tower, 531 Avenue of Americas, 1 Archer Avenue, and 160 Clarkson.

Prior to joining Whitehall, Janet worked at other reputable New York City firms, where she contributed to projects such as Pier 6 Brooklyn Bridge Park, 10 Nevins, and other various projects ranging from commercial to private residences.

Janet holds a Bachelor of Arts in Interior Design degree from Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City.

Lin Chen

Project Manager

As a Project Manager at Whitehall Interiors, Lin has over 6 years of experience in architecture and interior design with experience in large scale, multi-unit residential high rise and hospitality projects. He is involved in multiple phases of project delivery from design development to construction administration and oversees the office’s production standards. His recent projects include 101 Lincoln Avenue and 2401 Third Avenue in the Bronx, and 123 Linden Boulevard in Brooklyn.

Prior to Whitehall, Lin worked at other reputable New York firms, where he made contributions to a number of projects including the Renaissance New York Midtown Hotel and the Bryant Park Winter Village.

Lin holds a Bachelor of Architecture degree from Syracuse University.

Josh Rosenfeld

Lead Designer

As a Lead Designer at Whitehall Interiors, Josh has 7 years of experience in an array of private and large-scale residential and workplace projects in the US. He is involved from the onset of the project, establishing the overall concept and design direction, as well as programming, for each unique building. He manages the design process, successfully leading project teams from schematic design through design development. Josh is extensively involved as well from construction drawings through construction administration, ensuring that design intent and details are carried out. His most recent work includes 210 Clarkson Avenue, 202 East 23rd Street, 160 Clarkson Avenue, and 54 Crown Street.

Prior to joining Whitehall, Josh was a project designer at other multiple award-winning New York design firms and executed projects throughout the US.

Josh holds a Master of Interior Architecture degree from Rhode Island School of Design, and a Bachelor of Arts degree cum laude in History of Architecture, with a minor in Art History, from Syracuse University.

Erica Klopman

Senior Designer

As a Senior Designer at Whitehall Interiors, Erica brings 8 years of solid interior design experience, and is deeply involved in all aspects of the design process from initial concept development through construction administration and FF&E installation. She leads her teams with the understanding that design is a collaborative journey grounded in the interests of the clients. Her current work includes 2686 Broadway and 25-34 Jackson Avenue.

Prior to joining Whitehall, Erica held positions at other award winning design firms, where she successfully designed and managed large-scale distinguished hospitality brands, luxury multi-family and private residential projects throughout the US and abroad.

Erica holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Interior Design from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City.
